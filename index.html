About Me

Psychology Postgraduate & Mental Health Advocate

Hi! I’m Seraphy Ansita Bara, a 23-year-old postgraduate psychology student from Chanakya University, driven by curiosity about the human mind and passion for emotional wellbeing.

My fascination with psychology began during my medical exam prep and quickly grew into a lifelong mission. I’m especially captivated by how the brain works and the depth of mental disorders. Known for being cheerful, empathetic, and a great listener, I love being there for others—both inside and outside the classroom.

I find peace in music, nature, and the joy of cooking and baking. Psychology, to me, is more than a subject—it’s a way to connect, empower, and guide. I dream of helping students explore their identity and make confident, informed choices. Whether through counseling, research, or awareness-building, I strive to make mental health more human and accessible.

Seraphy Bara
